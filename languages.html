Languages

Learning a language is a valuable skill for any student's future in this increasingly globalised and multicultural world. Our online Zoom tutoring service offers modern foreign language courses in both French and Spanish. Our tutors have attained the highest grades at GCSE level in these subjects, and some have gone beyond, pursuing DELF qualifications to further enhance their proficiency. We focus on developing students' speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills, using our own experiences as learners to provide efficient and modern strategies to reach the top.
