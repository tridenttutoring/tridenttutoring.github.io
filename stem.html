STEM

At Trident Tutoring, we prepare students for the rapidly evolving world of STEM. We equip students with the skills to succeed in a variety of fields, including computer programming, software development and AI engineering. Our computer science tuition covers a range of popular languages such as Python, Swift, and JavaScript, as well as exam preparation. Our specialised tutors are experienced in engineering and technology. We also can provide courses in GCSE Physics, Chemistry and Biology. One feature of the top independent school is a willingness to foster lifelong passion among students, particularly in going beyond the syllabus. We aim to replicate this in our sessions, and the tutors' genuine interest in and knowledge of their subject areas allows students not only to succeed in exams, but also to learn more about the quickly expanding fields of STEM.
